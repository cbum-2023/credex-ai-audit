## FAQ

**Do I need to connect billing data?**  
No. The MVP uses manual inputs so founders can get an answer in minutes.

**Is the audit private?**  
The public link excludes email and company name. It shows tools, plans, and savings numbers only.

**Does the AI decide my savings?**  
No. The math is deterministic. AI only writes the summary paragraph.

**What happens if I am already optimized?**  
The report says so and offers a pricing-change notification signup.

**When should I talk to Credex?**  
If the audit finds more than $500/month in savings, discounted credits may be worth a procurement conversation.
